# GST Registration Guide for Snack Brand (SNAKRIO)

This document explains the **documents required and step-by-step process for GST Registration** in India, especially for starting a **ready-to-eat snack brand** like chips, makhana, or namkeen.

---

# 1. When GST Registration is Required

You must apply for GST registration if:

- Your annual turnover exceeds **₹40 lakh** (for goods in most states).
- You want to sell products on **online platforms or marketplaces**.
- You want to supply goods to **large retailers or distributors**.
- You want to issue **GST invoices** to customers.

For a snack brand selling through online platforms or quick-commerce services, **GST registration is usually mandatory** even if turnover is below the threshold.

---

# 2. Documents Required for GST Registration

## 1. Personal Documents
- PAN Card (mandatory)
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport-size photograph

## 2. Business Details
- Business name
- Business address
- Nature of business (Manufacturer / Trader)

## 3. Address Proof of Business Place
Any one of the following:

- Electricity bill
- Property tax receipt
- Rent agreement

If the place is rented:
- Rent agreement
- NOC (No Objection Certificate) from the owner

## 4. Bank Account Details
- Bank account number
- Cancelled cheque or bank statement

## 5. Digital Verification
- Aadhaar authentication (OTP)
- Mobile number and email verification

---

# 3. GST Registration Process

## Step 1
Visit the official GST portal: https://www.gst.gov.in/

## Step 2
Click on: Services → Registration → New Registration

## Step 3
Fill basic details:

- PAN number
- Mobile number
- Email ID
- State and district
- Business type

## Step 4
Verify using **OTP sent to mobile and email**.

You will receive a **Temporary Reference Number (TRN)**.

## Step 5
Login using the TRN and complete the full application.

Provide:

- Business details
- Promoter/owner details
- Business address
- Bank details
- Upload required documents

## Step 6
Submit the application using:

- Aadhaar authentication or
- Electronic verification code (EVC)

## Step 7
Application verification by GST department.

## Step 8
After approval, you will receive:

- **GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number)**  
- **GST Registration Certificate**

---

# 4. Structure of GSTIN

GSTIN is a **15-digit number**.

Example format: 09ABCDE1234F1Z5

Structure:

| Part | Meaning |
|-----|--------|
| 09 | State code |
| ABCDE1234F | PAN number |
| 1 | Entity number |
| Z | Default |
| 5 | Check code |

---

# 5. GST Rate for Snack Products

Common GST rates for snack products:

| Product | GST Rate |
|-------|--------|
| Namkeen / Bhujia | 12% |
| Chips | 12% |
| Roasted makhana | 5% |
| Packaged snacks | 12% |

Rates may vary depending on product category.

---

# 6. Benefits of GST Registration

- Legally sell products across India
- Sell on online marketplaces
- Claim **input tax credit**
- Build trust with retailers and distributors
- Required for many food delivery and quick commerce platforms

---

# 7. Time Required

Typical approval time:

- **3 to 7 working days** (if Aadhaar verification is completed)

---

# 8. Estimated Cost

If you apply yourself: Government Fee: ₹0

If applied through a CA or consultant: ₹1000 – ₹3000 (service fee)

---

# Conclusion

GST registration is an important step for scaling a food brand like **SNAKRIO**. Once registered, you can sell products legally across India and partner with retailers, distributors, and online platforms.
