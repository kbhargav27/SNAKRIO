# FSSAI Registration Guide for Snack Brand (SNAKRIO)

This guide explains the **documents required and the step-by-step process to obtain FSSAI registration** for starting a ready-to-eat snack brand such as chips, makhana, or namkeen.

---

# 1. Types of FSSAI License

Depending on your business turnover and scale, you must apply for one of the following licenses.

## 1. Basic FSSAI Registration
- Suitable for small food businesses
- Annual turnover: **Below ₹12 lakh**
- Ideal for **home-based or small snack manufacturing units**

## 2. State FSSAI License
- Annual turnover: **₹12 lakh – ₹20 crore**
- Required for **medium-sized food manufacturing businesses operating within one state**

## 3. Central FSSAI License
- Annual turnover: **Above ₹20 crore**
- Required for **large food manufacturers or businesses selling across multiple states**

For a **new snack brand like SNAKRIO**, most startups begin with:

- **Basic Registration** (small scale)
- **State License** (if planning larger production)

---

# 2. Documents Required for FSSAI Registration

## Basic Documents
- Aadhaar Card or PAN Card
- Passport-size photograph
- Mobile number
- Email address

## Business Address Proof
One of the following:
- Electricity bill
- Rent agreement
- Property tax receipt

## Manufacturing Details (for snack production)
- Food category (e.g., makhana, chips, namkeen)
- Manufacturing unit address
- Equipment list

Example equipment:
- Fryer
- Mixing machine
- Sealing machine
- Weighing machine

## If the place is rented
Additional documents required:
- Rent agreement
- No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the owner

---

# 3. FSSAI Registration Process

## Step 1
Visit the official FSSAI portal: https://foscos.fssai.gov.in/


## Step 2
Create an account on the FoSCoS portal.

## Step 3
Select: Apply for License / Registration


## Step 4
Fill the application form with the following details:
- Business name
- Business type (Manufacturer)
- Food category (Snacks)
- Business address

## Step 5
Upload the required documents.

## Step 6
Pay the registration fees.

### Approximate Fees

| License Type | Fee |
|---------------|------|
| Basic Registration | ₹100 per year |
| State License | ₹2,000 – ₹5,000 per year |

## Step 7
Application review by authorities.

## Step 8
Approval and license issuance.

You will receive a **14-digit FSSAI license number**.

This number **must be printed on every food package.**

---

# 4. Mandatory Information on Snack Packaging

Every packaged food product must include the following details:

- Brand name
- FSSAI license number
- Ingredient list
- Net weight
- Manufacturing date
- Expiry / Best Before date
- Maximum Retail Price (MRP)
- Manufacturer address
- Customer care contact number

---

# 5. Additional Registrations for Snack Brand

Besides FSSAI, it is recommended to obtain the following registrations:

## GST Registration
Required if selling through online marketplaces or large retailers.

## Trademark Registration
Protects your brand name (e.g., **SNAKRIO**).

## MSME / Udyam Registration
Helps access government benefits and subsidies.

## Shop & Establishment License
Required in some states for operating a commercial unit.

---

# 6. Requirements to Sell on Quick Commerce Platforms

If you plan to sell snacks on platforms like instant delivery apps, they usually require:

- Valid FSSAI license
- GST registration
- Brand authorization
- Product barcode (recommended)
- Proper packaging and labeling

---

# Conclusion

To start a snack brand like **SNAKRIO**, the essential first step is obtaining **FSSAI registration**. Once the license is obtained, you can proceed with packaging, branding, and selling your products through retail stores or online platforms.
