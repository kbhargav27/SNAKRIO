# 1. What is MSME / Udyam Registration

MSME (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises) registration gives **official recognition to small businesses** and provides benefits such as:

- Government schemes
- Subsidies on machinery
- Easier business loans
- Lower interest rates
- Protection against delayed payments

For a **snack manufacturing business**, the category will usually be: Manufacturing – Food Products

---

# 2. Documents Required for MSME Registration

The good news is that **very few documents are required**.

## Basic Documents

- **Aadhaar Card** of the owner
- **PAN Card** of the owner
- **Mobile number linked with Aadhaar**

## Business Information Required

You will need to provide:

- Business name  
- Business address  
- Type of organization (Proprietorship / LLP / Pvt Ltd)  
- Nature of business (Manufacturing / Service)  
- Bank account details  
- Number of employees  
- Investment in machinery  
- Annual turnover (approximate)

## Bank Details

- Bank account number
- IFSC code

No document upload is usually required because **data is verified automatically through PAN and Aadhaar**.

---

# 3. MSME Categories

MSME classification depends on **investment and annual turnover**.

| Category | Investment Limit | Turnover Limit |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|
| Micro Enterprise | Up to ₹1 crore | Up to ₹5 crore |
| Small Enterprise | Up to ₹10 crore | Up to ₹50 crore |
| Medium Enterprise | Up to ₹50 crore | Up to ₹250 crore |

A new snack brand like **SNAKRIO** will normally fall under: Micro Enterprise

---

# 4. MSME Registration Process

## Step 1
Go to the official MSME portal: https://udyamregistration.gov.in


## Step 2
Click: For New Entrepreneurs who are not Registered yet as MSME

---

## Step 3
Enter Aadhaar number and owner name.

Example: Owner Name: <owner-name>
Business Name: SNAKRIO FOODS

---

## Step 4
Verify Aadhaar using OTP sent to your mobile.

---

## Step 5
Enter PAN details for verification.

---

## Step 6
Fill business information:

- Business name
- Address
- Bank details
- Industry type
- Number of employees
- Investment amount

For your business select: Type of Enterprise: Manufacturing
                          Industry: Food Products / Snacks

---

## Step 7
Submit the application.

---

## Step 8
You will receive your **Udyam Registration Number (URN)** and certificate.

Example format: UDYAM-UP-00-0000000

---

# 5. Cost of MSME Registration Free


There is **no government fee** for Udyam registration.

---

# 6. Time Required

Registration is usually completed within: 10 – 15 minutes

The certificate is generated instantly.
